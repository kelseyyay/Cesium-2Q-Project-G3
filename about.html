About Us



Hello! We are Hit N Run, a group of Grade 9 students who took the challenge of creating a sports website for our Computer Science class. We initially believed the project would be enjoyable, but it was more than that. It required far more effort and late-night teamwork than we expected. Despite hundreds of coding errors, unexpected layout issues, and constant reminders within the group chat, we gained a deeper understanding of the importance of teamwork and cooperation in producing a successful website.


Our motto, “We Hit You with the News. We Run with the Truth” is exactly how we see this website. We want every article, every update, every highlight to be fast and honest, because if we’re going to share sports with you, we want you to feel it, not just read it. Hit N Run isn’t just a website, it’s us, figuring it all out as we go, making mistakes, laughing, celebrating the small wins, and sharing it all with you.
